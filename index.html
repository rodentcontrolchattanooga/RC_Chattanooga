How to Spot Them

Warning Signs of Mice & Rats in Your Home

Rodents are nocturnal and naturally evasive. Pay close attention to these vital environmental warnings to verify if action is immediately required.

Feces & Droppings

Finding small, dark cylindrical droppings near food pantries, under kitchen sinks, or along baseboards is the most explicit confirmation of active nesting.

Scuttling Noises

Noises within drywall, attics, or ceiling spaces—especially late at night when ambient room volume is low—frequently point to active nesting chambers.

Gnaw Marks & Damage

Rats and mice must chew constantly to maintain tooth health. Damages on wiring pathways, wood joists, structural piping, or food containers warrant speedy intervention.
